Jordan's Audio Visualizer

~ This is an audio visualization demo built using both - Web Audio API & Canvas API.

~ Click on the ? on the right at any time to return to this page.

~ Play with RGB color picture on top to interactively change the color of the visualizer!

~ Use the button on the right side of your page to upload your own mp3 tracks. Scroll to the bottom of the playlist section to view your track!

~ Simply just click on the track to play and pause the music!
